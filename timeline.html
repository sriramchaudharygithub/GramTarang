2012

Winner of Overall Best Performer award at NSDC partners meet, 2012. The National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), a Public Private Partnership of the Union Finance Ministry, introduced Annual Performance Awards to recognize the contribution made by NSDC Partners in transforming the skills landscape and help bridge the skills gap in the country. The first such Performance Awards for the financial year 2011-12 were given out by Executive Director in the Prime Ministers National Council on Skill Development, Mr JP Rai, and NSDC Chairman, Mr MV Subbiah, on the concluding day of the 3rd NSDC Partners Meeting in Delhi on June 09, 2012. Gram Tarang Employability Training Services was adjudged the Overall Best Performer during the last financial year. The jury took into account Gram Tarang presence in Naxal-affected areas within the geographies from where it operates and its impact on the local population for naming it the winner of the biggest award for exceeding its overall target for the financial year by training over 6,200 youth with a strong placement record of around 81% with over 75% of the candidates having SC/ST/OBC status and mostly originating from disadvantaged districts of Orissa & Andhra Pradesh
